Exeter Data Analytics Hub: Promoting Open and Reproducible Science

Welcome to the homepage of the Exeter Data Analytics Hub.

What is the Exeter Data Analytics Hub?

We are an inter-college team of volunteers at the University of Exeter aiming to enhance ECR training in open and reproducible science.

Open and Reproducible Science Series

We host a monthly series of workshops and talks to promote Open and Reproducible Science throughout the life-cycle of a research project.

Visit the Series page to find out more.
